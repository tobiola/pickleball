## Leagues

### **Honcho Pickleball League**
- ~$135 per person *(~$110 with code **Ola**)*
- 8-week season
- **Arch Pickleball** (near airport)
- Levels: 2.5 DUPR → 4.25 DUPR
- Formats:
  - Team doubles 3.0 DUPR to 3.5 DUPR **or**
  - Individual ladder 2.5 DUPR → 4.25 DUPR
- Prizes:
  - Free admission to next season 
  - Cash & gear for winners

[Register Here](https://honchopickleball.com/register/)

---

### **Paddle Up Pickleball Leagues**
- ~$110 per person
- Typically 6-week sessions
- Levels: 2.0 DUPR → 4.5 DUPR
- Multiple league options available

[View Leagues](https://www.paddleuppickleballclub.com/chesterfield)
